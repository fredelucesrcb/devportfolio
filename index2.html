Where I Came From

A Few Words on my past

I was born and raised in Surallah, South Cotabato. I had my education from elementary to High School at Notre Dame of Surala, garnering the Notre Damean of the Year award when I graduated.

Eventually, I took up Bachelor in Special Education major in Teaching the Gifted at West Visayas State University, La Paz, Iloilo City. I graduated Magna Cum Laude in 2015 and was awarded as the Most Outstanding Student Teacher in my curriculum

I also taught for two years at Ateneo de Iloilo-Santa Maria Catholic School-teaching fifth and sixth grade Social Studies, encompassing Philippine Hstory.

But alas, I felt that teaching is not my passion, hence, I enrolled at the Tuitt Coding Bootcamp to rekindle my love-hate (but mostly love) relationship with coding.
