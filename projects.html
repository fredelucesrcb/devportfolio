Gojira R34

Gojira R34 is a tribute page dedicated to the legendary Nissan Skyline GT-R34. The author envisions that the site will be a way to connect all those who share a passion for everything GT-R34. Through this site, enthusiasts, owners and fans in general can share their knowledge and love for the Gojira. Site is mobile-responsive

Languages Used:

  • HTML 5
  • CSS 3
  • Bootstrap 4
